Open Online Courses

Enables employees and associates, working with IETS and its subsidiary for more than 1 year, to opt for maximum 2 online programs in every financial year. This learning tool is being used to give its people the flexibility to choose relevant courses from a vast pool of programs available online covering variety of subjects

Note : For each approved course, the employee will be reimbursed 50% of the course fee OR $100, whichever is less, on submission of the completion certificate

Process: The learner shall take prior permission from HR before enrolling, by submitting the course identified and rationale for choosing the same. HR along with the Business Head, shall assess the credibility of the course and its intended benefits before communicating its approval. We shall continuously support the learners to source programs matching their aspirations

Recommended online training platforms are as below:

  1. Coursera :https://www.coursera.org/
  2. EDX: https://www.edx.org
  3. Harvard University: http://online-learning.harvard.edu/courses
  4. MOOCS (Massive Open Online Courses): https://www.thecompleteuniversityguide.co.uk/distance-learning/moocs-(massive-open-online-courses)
  5. Open2Study: https://www.open2study.com/

While the above are only indicative, employees are encouraged to explore other such online platforms and share details, if useful and relevant, for the benefit of a larger audience

Organized Trainings

Include trainer led classroom and blended programs identified in consultation with Business Heads and Department Heads and are compulsory to attend for the identified learners

These trainings have been bucketed into

  1. Generic trainings will be conducted basis the learner segmentation into front line staff, Supervisor, Manager and Business Leader
  2. Targeted tranings are job role based trainings. These will be developed and conducted with the aim to enhance the functional knowledge and capability of individuals in identified job roles
  3. Management Development Programs (MDP) include uniquely designed courses for the leadership team and aims to equip the leadership to build a high performance culture

Process: iLEARN team shall work closely with business teams to refer to the goals and objectives of each department, understand knowledge, skills and attitude required for each job role and facilitate development and delivery of courses identified. The iLEARN team shall collect post training feedback to enhance the efficacy of the programs year on year
