Local Conveyance

Policy

The policy aims to reimburse its employees a local conveyance for all official travel undertaken as per the work requirements.

Eligibility

The policy applies to all permanent employees, & temporary employees associated with IETS.

Policy Specifications

Entitlements for local Conveyance

  • There shall be no conveyance reimbursement to employees in the grade of Senior Manager & above for official travel within their city of posting.
  • Conveyance reimbursement for resources in the grade of Manager & below will be Rs. 2.50/km irrespective of the fact they are using 4 wheeler or 2 wheeler for local conveyance.
  • For official travel within city all employees are expected to travel by their personal vehicle or by public cabs like Meru, Ola, Uber etc.

Kindly note: it will be management’s endeavor to ensure economization of the costs for local conveyance. Employees are encouraged to utilize the most cost effect choice available while choosing local conveyance.

Other Terms
  • In case of non-availability of default mode of transport, employees must take approval from superior/ branch head for an alternate mode of transport.
  • Female employees, in case of odd timings such as late sitting beyond 8:30 p.m. or early morning before 8:00 a.m., may avail services of private taxis (like Meru, Forshe etc.) or cool cabs as a measure of security
  • Employees shall submit bills, duly approved by their respective supervisors/ Branch Heads, of private hired taxis along with the conveyance reimbursement form / lotus forms.
  • For reimbursements against own vehicles, employees shall give a declaration to accounts with details of destination and distance covered.
  • While on official travel expenses like lunch, snacks and others may be claimed on actuals, supported by vouchers.
  • Female Employee who have to catch early morning (6.00 A.M & before) flight / train may hire cool cab / Meru or something similar from residence to Airport Terminal / Station.
