Guest House Policy

Policy

Company maintains guest house at different locations as temporary transit accommodation for those who are visiting those location for official purposes. Guest House facility can also be used by those employees who have been transferred or appointed for a fixed period as enumerated below.

Eligibility

The policy applies to all permanent employees, temporary employees and management trainees associated with IETS.

Policy Specifications
  • The following category of employees are eligible to stay in the Guest House.
    • All employees of the company in the grade of Officer & above subject to following:
      • In case of employees who have been transferred they will be allowed to stay for a maximum period of four weeks.
      • New joinees will also be entitled to stay in the G / H for a maximum period of four weeks.
      • Employees employed or posted in the city where G/H is located shall under no circumstances be allowed to stay in the guesthouse.
    • Company Guest. Reservation for Company Guest can only be made with the approval of location– Head with intimation to Corporate Admin - Head for a duration not exceeding three days.
  • Employee who is on private visit are not eligible to stay in the G / H. However in case of medical emergency employee with the special permission of Corporate HR & Admin department may be allowed to stay for a period not exceeding two weeks.
Register Maintance:

The following registers needs to be compulsorily maintained in all G / H

  • Guest Movement Register
  • Purchase Register
  • Complaint Register

It will be the responsibility of concerned location head to ensure all rules & regulations as enumerated above are followed. He will also be required to send monthly report to Corporate Admin, giving details of Guest Movement and G /H running costs for the month.

This G / H policy will be applicable with immediate effect.

All employees who are currently using / staying in the G /H but are not eligible for the same as per the rules & regulations of this policy are requested to vacate the G /H within one month.

Any changes to this policy would be entertained with the explicit permission of the competent authority.

Employees seeking voluntary transfers will not be covered by any of the above, and will have to bear their own expenses.
