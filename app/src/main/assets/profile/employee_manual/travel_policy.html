The Company has been following a need based car hire policy for executive travelling to other cities. Based on feedback received from many of the locations, it is evident that in almost all the cities where we have our offices, there is facility of cool cabs / city taxies / metered taxies which work out to be economical without compromising on convenience.

It is, therefore, recommended that all executives travelling to different cities need to use one of the above suggested methods and should not hire the car for a day. Car hire would be permitted only in case the employees are required to visit a number of offices of different clients during the same day and are planning to return the same day, with the approval of Group/Dept Head.

4. Ticket - Booking
  • All tickets (Air & Train) will be booked centrally by the travel desk. If booking for business exigency reason is done by the employee on his own, cost of the ticket shall be reimbursed post supervisor approval only
  • Only employees in VP Grade & above will be allowed to travel by air. However Group / Dept Heads at their discretion can allow employees in the grade of AVP and below to travel by air, only in the event of any business exigency.
  • Request for preponement of air ticket will not be accepted. On account of this company has to bear not only cancellation charges but also difference in fare between the old booking and new booking. Travel Desk will not accept any request for such preponement of air ticket, without the respective Group / Dept Head approval.
  • Travel Desk will book the ticket on the Airlines having cheapest fare. Any request for booking ticket on specific Airlines will not be entertained.
  • Train tickets will be booked as per seat availability (2nd or 3rd AC).
5. Other Rules i. Conditions Applicable to all Travel:

All hotel booking shall be done centrally by the travel desk as per one's eligibility. Hotel stay will be strictly as per entitlement.

  • In the case of employees travelling to locations where a Guest House exists, employees would in the first instance be booked at the Guest House. However, if the Guest House is not available, then with prior approval, they would be booked at a hotel by the local office
  • Any expenses in excess of the above approved limits; or any deviations from the above rules to be sanctioned by the concerned supervisor, and also to be further ratified by the supervisor’s superior. All expense claims will have to be routed through Accounts before being approved by the concerned superiors so as to adhere to the limits and rules laid down
  • Reimbursement of conveyance expenses would be done on the basis of actuals. This would include travel between place of stay and place of work and any other travel undertaken for business purposes
  • While on tour, long distance communication expenses from the hotel should be restricted to the bare minimum and in case of urgent communication with IETS offices, the concerned office should be asked to call back
  • No reimbursement will be made for hard drinks unless this is an expense incurred for business promotion and is specifically authorised by the Approving Authority (only SVP and above are authorised to approve the same). The name of the guest entertained must be mentioned on the bill and the same should accompany the travel expense voucher
  • In case of outstation travel all employees are expected to have cabs from the airport/railway station for local travel within the city being visited. Booking of cabs for full day can only be done with the prior approval of Group/Dept Head.
  • In the case of airport drops and pick-ups, only lady employees who depart / arrive by flights departing / arriving at abnormal hours will be permitted to hire a car for the purpose
ii. Conditions Specific to Long Duration Tour (15 nights and more upto 90 days):

Hotel stay will be strictly as per entitle¬ment. However, if for some reason the employees are unable to stay at the Group Company Guest House, they can, with prior approval, choose an alternative hotel convenient to them, within the following entitlements:

  • Accommodation: Lodging for the employee to be provided preferably at a Group Company Guest House or if that is not possible, then with prior approval, at a hotel as per the grade entitlement
  • Spouse: The employee’s spouse may be allowed to join the employee in case of a long duration tour or in cases where the employee is temporarily relocated for a period beyond 30 days. However, this would not be an entitlement, but by prior approval of the Competent Authority. In case the spouse is so allowed: - In the case of a long-duration tour: The facilities regarding lodging, boarding and travel would be as approved in advance by the Competent Authority
  • In the case of temporary relocation for a period beyond 30 days:
    • Accommodation expenses incurred for putting up of the spouse at the hotel or guesthouse for 2 days would be borne by the Company. Hotel as per employee's eligibility
    • One-time Return Economy Class Air fare for employee's spouse and children from home station to the city the employee has been temporarily relocated would be borne by the Company
    • For tours extending beyond a period of 90 days each case would be reviewed afresh, and an approv¬al would need to be taken from the Competent Authority
