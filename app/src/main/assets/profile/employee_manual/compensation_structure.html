Compensation Structure

Policy

The policy orients the team members to the prevailing Compensation structure at IETS.

Eligibility

The policy applies to all permanent employees associated with IETS.

Policy Specifications

The IETS compensation structure has following components:

  1. Salaried Components
  2. Annual Benefit
  3. Retiral Benefits
  4. Other Benefits
Components Component Details
Salaried Components (Form a part of the monthly salary slip.)
Basic Salary/ Consolidated Pay not to exceed 20% of the CTC
Variable House Rent Allowance (VHRA) @ 50% Of Basic Salary
Conveyance Allowance/reimbursement * As % of Basic Salary
@ 75% – Secretary to AVP
@ 85% - VP to SVP/ Advisor / COO
City Compensatory Allowance Balance amount
Reimbursement Component (Paid as part of pay slip component). Tax free in the hands of the employee if supporting invoices submitted to accounts.
Medical Reimbursement Rs.1,250 per month
Phone Reimbursement (Mobile or Landline) As per grade entitlement Available To Select Grade Levels
Annual Benefit
Leave Travel Allowance (LTA) Applicable after confirmation & completion of one year of service. As per Grade Entitlement.
Retiral Benefit
Provident Fund @ 12% Of Basic Salary. Voluntary contribution can be made upto 24%.
Superannuation @ 13% of Basic Salary (for employees
who have joined before April 1, 2005)
*Superannuation has been discontinued
w.e.f April 1, 2005. All employees joining post
April 1, 2005 are not entitled to this component.
Gratuity Applicable after 5 years of continuous service, @15 days of Basic Salary per year of service
Other Benefits (available to employees over and above the salary and reimbursements)
Medi-claim Insurance Coverage set grade wise
Group Term & Personal Accident Insurance Coverage set grade wise
Special Medical Distress Reimbursement Applicable to all employees
Salary Advance An employee may apply for a temporary advance with the approval of the HOD & HR Head. Temporary advance upto three months' salary (i.e. Cost to the Company), may be availed. The same may be recovered in a maximum of 12 monthly instalments. This advance above Rs. One Lac, will be an interest bearing advance. The interest shall be based on the borrowing cost of the Company as advised by the Group CFO from time to time. This facility shall be taxable as per prevalent Income Tax Rules.
Advance amount upto Rs. 5 Lacs OR six months of salary (i.e. Cost to the Company), whichever is lower may be provided with the approval of Managing Director.
Corporate Gift for Marriage @ Rs. 5,000/-
