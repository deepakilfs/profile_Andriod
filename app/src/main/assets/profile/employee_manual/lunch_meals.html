Lunch / Meals

Policy

The policy aims to reimburse its employees lunch expenses for all official working days at IETS.

Eligibility

The policy applies to all permanent employees, temporary employees and management trainees associated with IETS.

Other Terms
  • In case of places where lunch coupons are not available, employees shall be given Lunch allowance of an equivalent amount along the monthly salary. However, the lunch allowance will be taxable as per the IT rules. The request for the same will have to be routed through the HR Department.
