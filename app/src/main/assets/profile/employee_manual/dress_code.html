Dress Code

Policy

The policy provides guidance to employees on the aesthetic presentation of employees while conducting official duty.

Eligibility

The policy applies to all permanent employees, temporary employees and management trainees associated with IETS.

Terms & Conditions
  • Employees shall dress to reflect IETS’ culture, values and industry.
  • Employees shall be expected to be dressed in modest and clean clothing at all times which makes them feel comfortable and at the same time presentable.
  • Dress code, for all working days, will be formal wear for gentlemen and either sarees, salwar suits or formal office wear for ladies.
  • Employees must refrain from wearing Jeans, T-shirts, flashy colors or any clothing that is likely to convey an adverse image of IETS.
  • The dress code on weekends (Saturdays & Sundays) and holidays only, may be casual. Employees are also urged to observe decorum at work and to desist from unnecessary, unseemly, loud and boisterous behavior.
