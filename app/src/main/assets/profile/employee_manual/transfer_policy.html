Transfer Policy

Reimbursement of Expenses Transfer

Any employee of IETS who is being transferred from one city to another at the Company’s behest will be entitled to two days leave with full pay and the following transfer allowances:

  • MODE: Travel eligibility for employee and his family would be as follows from the present city to the city of transfer
    GradeEligibility
    Officer – Asst ManagerAC III
    Mgr – Sr.ManagerAC II
    AVP & AboveAir
  • PACKING AND UNPACKING EXPENSES AND TRANSPORTATION OF BAGGAGE: These shall be paid / reimbursed at actuals. The arrangement for the same are required to be made compulsorily through the Administration Department
  • COST OF TRANSPORTING VEHICLE: This shall be reimbursed based on actuals. If the vehicle is being driven, then reimbursement would be at the rate of Rs. 4/- per km. If the employee needs to stay overnight at a hotel, then hotel accommodation would be as per entitlement for him and his family and he would also be entitled to a per day allowance as applicable to his grade
  • ACCOMMODATION: When an employee is transferred from one city to another to meet Company objectives, irrespective of the level of the employee the Company will provide suitable accommodation, to be decided by the Competent Authority subject to maximum stay of seven nights.
  • TRANSFER GRANT: Any employee of IETS who is being transferred from one city to another at the Company’s behest will be entitled to a transfer grant as per table given below.
  • Taking into account the hardship an employee and his family has to bear on account of relocation it is proposed to substitute monthly relocation allowance with one time transfer grant. The limits proposed are:
    Grade Grant for relocation with family Grant for relocation without family
    VP & AboveRs. 1,00,000Rs. 33,000
    SM to AVPRs. 75,000Rs. 24,750
    Manager & BelowRs. 50,000Rs. 16,500

Employees seeking voluntary transfers will not be covered by any of the above, and will have to bear their own expenses.
