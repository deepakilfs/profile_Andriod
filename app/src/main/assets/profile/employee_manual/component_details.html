Component Details

Salaried Components
Basic Salary/ Consolidated Pay

Basic salary is set on a case to case basis and forms the base for calculating other allowances including VHRA, Conveyance allowance, Retirals (PF, Superannuation & Gratuity) and other benefits such as leave encashment, etc. Please refer to the respective policies to understand the calculation formulae and associated terms and conditions. Basic Salary is fully taxable as per the IT Act.

Conveyance Allowance /Reimbursement

Conveyance Allowance has been provided to all grades as per entitlements:

Grade Entitlement
(as a % of Basic Salary)
COO/Senior Vice President/ Advisor / Vice President 85%
Assistant Vice President to Officer 75%

Employees also have the option to claim the conveyance allowance as reimbursement. To opt for reimbursement, a request has to be sent to HR. By default the Conveyance Allowance is included in the salary, unless otherwise specified. The bills for reimbursement would include fuel expenses, parking charges, driver’s salary, lease rentals and repairs & maintenance expenses.

City Compensatory Allowance

City Compensatory Allowance is a fully taxable component of the cost to company and is paid as balancing figure.

Reimbursements

All reimbursements are tax free in the hands of the employees, provided they present appropriate bills while claiming reimbursements.

Medical Reimbursement

Employees shall be entitled to reimbursement of medical expenses not covered under the Mediclaim policy (Refer to Medical Benefits Policy on Page: C – 32). This is paid as part of monthly salary @ Rs.1,250 per month wef April 1, 2013. In case the employee joins during the month, the medical reimbursement entitlements will be provided on a pro-rata basis

Depending on business exigencies, employees in the grade Officer to Senior Manager may claim reimbursement of mobile expenses subject to the approval of the reporting authority and/or Department Head. Gratuity

Employees, on leaving the service after completion of continuous service of five years, shall be eligible to Gratuity @ 15 days of basic salary as on the date of leaving for every completed year of service or part thereof in excess of 6 months, subject to the maximum limit specified in the Payment of Gratuity Act amended from time to time. The employee will not be entitled to any benefits if he leaves the service before completing five years of service.

Superannuation

Superannuation is applicable for only those employees who joined IETS before April 1, 2005 and is paid @ 13% of the CP. The superannuation is not applicable for employees who are covered under the Bonus Act. The contribution will be made to the Fund, and will be governed by the rules to be framed by the Competent Authority.
